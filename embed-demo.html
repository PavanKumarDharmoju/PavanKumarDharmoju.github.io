Virtual CRISPR: Can LLMs Predict CRISPR Screen Results?

Steven Song, Abdalla Abdrabou, Asmita Dabholkar, Kastan Day, Pavan Dharmoju, Jason Perera, Volodymyr Kindratenko, Aly A. Khan
Proceedings of the 24th Workshop on Biomedical Language Processing (BioNLP 2025)
CRISPR-Cas systems enable systematic investigation of gene function, but experimental CRISPR screens are resource-intensive. Here, we investigate the potential of Large Language Models (LLMs) to predict the outcomes of CRISPR screens in silico, thereby prioritizing experiments and accelerating biological discovery.

SEO Strategy Analysis

✅ Custom Embed (Best for SEO)

  • • Content is indexable by search engines
  • • Structured data markup possible
  • • Fast page load times
  • • Mobile-friendly design
  • • Full control over presentation

⚠️ Proxy iFrame (Moderate SEO)

  • • Content may not be indexed
  • • Potential loading delays
  • • Works around CORS issues
  • • Preserves original formatting
  • • Limited mobile optimization

❌ Direct iFrame (Poor SEO)

  • • Content not indexed by search engines
  • • Often blocked by X-Frame-Options
  • • Poor mobile experience
  • • No structured data benefits
  • • Dependent on external site
